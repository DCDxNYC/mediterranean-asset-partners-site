The Structure

How ownership
works on-chain

01
Legal Wrapper
Property is placed into a Cyprus Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV). The SPV holds legal title. Token holders own equity or income rights within the SPV — not the land directly.
02
Token Issuance
Smart contracts mint ERC-20 tokens against the SPV on issuance. Each token is a digital representation of your ownership stake, transferable and tradeable between whitelisted wallets.
03
KYC & Compliance
All investors complete KYC/AML verification. Token transfers are restricted to verified, whitelisted addresses — maintaining CySEC and EU regulatory compliance at all times.
04
Yield Distributions
Rental income flows from tenants → SPV → smart contract → token holder wallets. Fully transparent, auditable on-chain. No intermediaries. No opacity.

Why Cyprus

A jurisdiction
built for capital

EU
Full EU Membership
Property rights governed under European law. Investor protections backed by EU frameworks, MiFID regulation, and an independent judiciary with English common law roots.
TAX
Low Tax Environment
12.5% corporate tax rate — one of the lowest in the EU. Extensive double tax treaty network covering 65+ countries. No inheritance tax. No capital gains on securities.
GEO
Strategic Location
Natural bridge between European, MENA, and Eastern Mediterranean capital flows. A proven jurisdiction for cross-border structuring and international investment vehicles.
RWA
RWA-Ready Regulation
CySEC is among the most progressive EU regulators on digital assets. Cyprus provides a clear legal pathway for tokenized real estate under existing securities frameworks.
